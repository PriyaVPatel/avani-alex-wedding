Our Story

It started with an open door

Avani and Alex met at the University of Maryland on a snowy February morning in 2017, the kind of morning campus probably should have been closed, except they both had a 6 AM exam. Avani arrived at 5 AM to study and found herself locked out in the freezing cold, spending a full fifteen minutes trying to catch the attention of the one person inside. Alex finally looked up, and opened the door.

Walking past, she noticed he was studying from the very same book. Same class, same exam. She invited him to join her study group; he politely passed, and she told him the offer stood. Before long he made his way over, and the rest is history. Study sessions became workouts, friendship blossomed into love, and the answer was yes twice: first when Alex asked her out, and again when he proposed.

Since then, Alex earned his doctorate in physical therapy, Avani launched her professional mehndi career, and together they bought their first home in Maryland.

Flying in

  • Reagan National (DCA) — recommended. Closest and easiest: about 20–25 min by car, or ~35–40 min by Metro with one transfer.
  • Dulles (IAD) — roughly 40–55 min by car depending on traffic; Silver Line Metro takes over an hour.
  • Baltimore (BWI) — roughly 55–75 min by car; MARC train + Metro is possible but slower.

Plan buffer time — D.C. traffic is real, especially on Friday afternoons.

Metro & parking

The hotel is a ~5 minute walk from the Woodley Park–Zoo / Adams Morgan station on the Red Line — an easy ride from Union Station and downtown.

Paid parking is available at the hotel, with street parking across from the hotel depending on availability. Rideshares drop right at the main entrance.
